How to Get Started as a Student

Step 1: Set Up Your Employer Account

Register your company on Nexternship and fill in your profile details. Let students know what you offer and why they should apply to your opportunities.

Step 2: Post Workshops with Detailed Descriptions

BCreate informative workshop posts that clearly outline the skills, qualifications, and learning opportunities you are offering. This helps attract the right candidates.

Step 3: Engage with Applicants

Review applications as they come in. Communicate directly with students through email, provide feedback, and choose the candidates best suited for your workshops.

Job Posting Features

You can edit your existing job postings, delete them, and view your accepted/rejected applicants.
